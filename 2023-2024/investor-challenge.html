INVESTOR CHALLENGE

Are you intrigued by the world of finance and investing? Here’s an exciting opportunity to explore financial markets in a risk-free environment.

This challenge allows you to experience the thrill of trading stocks, options, and other instruments without risking any real money. It’s a unique chance to develop your analytical skills, understand market dynamics, and make strategic investment decisions, all while having fun and learning valuable lessons.

To add some friendly competition, prizes will be awarded to the top three participants with the highest-ranking portfolios, and their names will be listed on Economics Quarterly’s website. However, the true value lies in the experience itself – the knowledge gained, the critical thinking developed, and the insight into the fascinating world of finance.

The goal of this challenge is to provide an engaging and educational platform for students to explore their interests in investing, without any material risk. Whether you’re a seasoned trader or a curious newcomer, this is your chance to dive into the financial markets and embark on an enriching journey.

Don’t miss out on this unique opportunity to gain practical experience, sharpen your skills, and discover your passion for finance.
